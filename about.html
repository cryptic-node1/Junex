Our Story

From Corporate Agencies to Personal Branding

Junex started when we realized a massive gap in the web design industry. Massive agencies catered to huge corporations, while individual professionals—doctors, engineers, and consultants—were left struggling with clunky, generic template builders that failed to represent their true expertise.

We founded Junex to bring agency-level design, conversion optimization, and modern technology specifically to the individual. We believe that your personal brand is your most valuable asset, and your website should reflect that perfectly.
