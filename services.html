Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

Typically, a Profile page takes 1 week, a Portfolio takes 2-3 weeks, and a Service Business website takes 3-4 weeks from the moment we have all your content.

Do I need to provide the domain and hosting?

Yes, you will maintain ownership of your domain and hosting. However, we guide you through the process of purchasing them and handle all the technical setup.

Can I update the content myself later?

Absolutely. We build our sites on user-friendly platforms (or provide custom CMS solutions) and include a 1-hour handover training session so you can edit text and images easily.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer optional care plans starting at $49/month which include security updates, backups, and minor content changes.
